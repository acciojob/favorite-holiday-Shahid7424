Maldives

The Maldives is a tropical paradise known for its stunning white-sand beaches, crystal-clear waters, and vibrant coral reefs. It's the perfect destination for a relaxing and luxurious getaway.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. Snorkeling at Banana Reef: Explore the diverse marine life and colorful corals.
  2. Relaxing at Veligandu Island Beach: Unwind on the pristine beaches and enjoy the breathtaking sunsets.
  3. Visiting Malé Hukuru Miskiy: Discover the historic Malé Friday Mosque, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  4. Admiring the Underwater Sculpture Museum: Experience the unique art and marine conservation project.
  5. Exploring Bioluminescent Beaches: Witness the magical natural phenomenon of bioluminescent plankton at Vaadhoo Island.

Must-Try Foods

  • Mas Huni: A traditional Maldivian breakfast dish made with tuna, coconut, and onions.
  • Fihunu Mas: Grilled fish marinated in rich spices, a staple in Maldivian cuisine.
  • Bis Keemiya: Deep-fried pastries filled with fish, coconut, and spices, a popular snack.
  • Gulha: Savory fish balls with coconut, chili, and garlic, a flavorful street food delight.
  • Boshi Mashuni: A refreshing salad made with finely chopped banana flower, coconut, and lime.
